About BeepBoop

BeepBoop is, in this first iteration, a pretty simple DAW (Digital Audio Workstation) with two instrument types available: an analog-style synthesizer and a drum machine. You can make patterns combining any number of these, arrange them in the timeline, and export to WAV. You can even use a USB MIDI keyboard!

Future iterations will be more fully functional. Plans include: the ability to login/save your work/collaborate with other users, sample-based instruments like samplers and granular synths, recording your own audio, and more.

The app is built in Angular 7 and uses Tone.js to generate sound via the Web Audio API. It is optimized for Chrome and because of compatibility issues with the Web Audio API, you may encounter issues on mobile, Safari, and older browsers.
