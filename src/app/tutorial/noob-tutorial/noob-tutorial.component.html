So You Want To Learn How To Beep Boop

Congratulations! Get ready to be That Weird Dude in your friend group who's obsessed with synthesizers (for the record, I think Dude is gender-neutral). The rabbit hole of electronic noise goes deep my friend, and you can spend a lifetime traversing it without knowing everything.

This tutorial assumes you know a little something about music- basic rhythms, time signatures, tempos, and the harmonic series. If you don't, as with anything these days they're a simple google away.

Much of this information is the same as an undergraduate course I taught on this exact topic at the University of Colorado-Boulder.
